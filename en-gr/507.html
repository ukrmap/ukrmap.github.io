Background

the territory of Rivne region traces of human occupation belongs to the ancient times. In the II-III century. The early Slavic tribes lived here Zarubyntsi Cherniahivs'ka and cultures. About 100 residues of ancient settlements. In the X century. these lands belonged to Kievan Rus. Dorogobuzh and Peresopnytsya (now village) in XI-XII centuries. were the centers of autonomous principalities. In XIII-XIV centuries. Rivne region was part of Galicia-Volyn principality, while the second half of XIV century. Lithuania fell under the power. After the Union of Lublin in 1569 - Under Polish rule. In the XVI century. important political and cultural center was the city jail. Near the village. Pliasheva summer 1651 held Berestetskaya battle between Cossack army Khmelnitskogo and Poles. Before 1793 region was under Polish rule, then - Russia. In 1921 Rivne went to Poland as part of Volyn province. In 1939, - Part of USSR. During World Rivne war was the administrative center Reichscommissariat "Ukraine".
