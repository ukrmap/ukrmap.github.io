Student projects

Working in small groups, gather information to prepare presentation on "process. Influence of economic modernization,  political and social life of the region at the beginning of XX century. "for these project tasks.

Working on tasks, and use reference scientific and popular literature, works of fiction that you studied in class or read independently, Internet sources. Important for you to be and family archives, photo documents, memories witnesses, the funds of the local museum.


Group 1

Working with a map, determine there industrial centers in your area, the fastest growing in the early XX century., illustrate the growth of individual  industries and agriculture that developed in the province  in these times.

On the basis of maps and literature prepare the story of industrialization in the province and its impact on the living area.

On the basis of maps and literature describe migration processes that affected the population of your country in the early twentieth century.

Prepare a computer presentation and oral report on the results of your research.

Economic development of Ukrainian lands in the early twentieth century.

Group 2

Working with literature and other sources, specify:

  • features of the modernization of economic and social life in the early XX century. in your land;
  • influence the processes of modernization in the region's economy and living standards;
  • impact of economic crisis 1900 - 1903 th on the life of the population;
  • changes in agricultural land in connection with the Stolypin agrarian reform.

Prepare a computer presentation and oral report on the results of your research.

Ukraine in 1907-1914 biennium

Group 3

Working with literature and other sources, specify:

  • key processes that characterized the social and political life of the region at the beginning of XX century.;
  • main directions and tasks of associations, organizations, political parties that existed in your land;
  • famous countrymen - active participants in the political life of the region.

Prepare a computer presentation and oral report on the results of your research.

Social and political life of Ukrainian lands in the early twentieth century.

Group 4

Working with maps and literature, specify:

  • place in your area related to the events of 1905-1907 revolution
  • Facts on the deployment of the national movement, the fight of workers and peasants, unrest in the army or the navy in your land;
  • consequences of the Revolution 1905 -1907 biennium for your region.
Prepare a computer presentation and oral report on the results of your research.  

Ukraine in the First Russian Revolution of 1905-1907

Group 5

Working with literature and other sources, specify:

  • changes in the daily life of inhabitants of your land under the influence of modernization processes;
  • differences between such changes in the city and the countryside.

Prepare a computer presentation and oral report on the results of your research.

Group 6

Working with literature and other sources, specify:

  • changes in higher education;
  • changes in science and culture;
  • prominent science or culture, who lived in the region or were born with it.

Prepare a computer presentation and oral report on the results of your research.
