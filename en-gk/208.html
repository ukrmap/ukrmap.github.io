World (compendium)

With maps we can determine the geographical position of Ukraine. Our state is in the Northern Hemisphere relative to the equator in the Eastern Hemisphere and relatively Prime Meridian on the mainland Eurasia.

Ukraine - European country whose neighbors are Russia, Belarus, Poland, Slovakia, Hungary, Romania and Moldova. The size of the territory of our country in Europe behind only Russia. And as for the population is the sixth after Russia, Germany, France, Italy, Great Britain. Black Sea to the south separates our country from Bulgaria, Turkey and Georgia. Ukraine Dnipro River crosses from north to south, dividing it into two similar size pieces.

capital of Ukraine is Kyiv - city of the 1500-year history.

climate, advantageous geographical location, high scientific and technological potential and hardworking people is the guarantee that Ukraine could soon become one of the richest countries in Europe.
