Water (compendium)

Dnieper and Desna are major sources of water supply for Kyiv. However, the water in these rivers heavily polluted by industrial effluents, especially as her drinking worsened after the Chernobyl accident. To solve the problem of drinking water in several dozen built artesian wells. The water here gets deep with hundred meters and is well cleaned, passed through filters of natural rocks.
