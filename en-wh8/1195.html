1. Arts Features Revival

European culture of this period was divided into two eras - Renaissance and Baroque. Cradle of Renaissance art, or Renaissance, considered Italy. It reopened Italian masters the art of ancient Greece and Ancient Rome, whence comes the name and age. The new art emerged based on humanism. Humanists claimed the highest value of human thought she has undeniable right to free development and implementation of their opportunities. Outstanding masters Renaissance was characterized by their versatility gifted: they combine with painting, architecture, sculpture, literature, philosophy with the study of exact sciences. Creative Masters Renaissance laid the foundations of European culture of the modern age.

Supreme flourishing Renaissance art reached during the High Renaissance (late XV - The First Decade XVI cent.) - "Golden Age" Italian literature, fine arts, philosophy and science. These were Titans during the Renaissance, that laid the foundation for a new understanding of the world and human place in it. Under the influence of Italian Renaissance art in the spread Nordic countries. Here, the formation of a new culture of decisive influence was not an ancient heritage and Christianity.

The Renaissance initiating significant changes in the outlook of people, their religious and political convictions. Began a new type of person who was more rozkripachenoyu spiritually and convinced myself than before. New ideas of making its way in the fight against official religious teachings, preparing a revolution in outlook People on 3 years of the Reformation. That is why this period is called the epoch Renaissance and Reformation.

2. Leonardo da Vinci(1452-1519) was born in Florence. In the age of 14 he began to study painting. However, this did not stop - Lifelong hard studied various sciences. Perhaps no area of human knowledge, which would not interest Leonardo. He was asked in mathematics, anatomy, different technologies, building aircraft and submarines, in his account, you can find descriptions helicopter, parachute, car, poison gas, phone, etc.. Perpetuity his knowledge was amazing then and now.

Leonardo-painter worked very slowly, constantly searching, experimenting. Painting was for it means knowing the world. He gazed into the faces of people trying to understand their inner world and bring in their paintings. In general, he wrote about half a dozen pictures, but each of them was a consummate work of high art.

In one of its early works "Madonna with flower Leonardo abandoned the usual image sad and wistful Madonna described her cheerful, full of women beauty. In this film he first used svitotin for "revival" of the object.

In the heyday her talent Leonardo created a fresco on the Biblical theme of "The Last Supper" (1495-1497 biennium). The basis of murals - the gospel story of how during Supper Christ before his execution said students that one of them betrays him. Expressions Face of Christ's disciples are filled with indignation, surprise, horror and perverse face of Judas handed with an impressive depth of realism.

One of became the most famous works of Leonardo portrait of Mona Lisa, the wife of a merchant del Gioconda. This work is considered to be the first in Western European painting psychological portrait. Painter has managed to focus the viewer is on the outside attractiveness of the Mona Lisa, and her internal world. Peering into her elderly face, feel the will and intellect, it seems that is not considered by the viewer, she barely hidden smile is following him. Now this picture is in Paris Louvre Museum. In this picture zakohuvalys, her hated by her kinchaly suicide, but finally to understand and unravel its secrets so and failed.

Leonardo was proud and independent. Throughout life poneviryavsya not wanting to serve anyone, and died in exile in France May 2, 1519

In his great marble "David" five-meter height of it, contrary to biblical mifovi, described the boy did not, and blossoming young man full of titanic forces. This statue was also a return ancient symbol of beauty in art, and an ideal new man, embodiment of unshakable will and power.

4. Raphael(1483-1520). He called the light of the genius of Italian Renaissance, which managed to combine the spirit of antiquity with the spirit of Christianity. During his short life Rafael was able to do very much like an artist. In his works he reproduced subtle human emotions, spiritual and physical beauty of the characters. This new ideal of man he first affirmed in numerous portraits and images of Madonna. The peak of creativity Raphael believe created in 1513 "Sistine Madonna". An interesting composition is a picture: curtain opens, and we see a woman holding a baby before, the woman at the same time gives the child, and wants to keep her in her look - sadness, anxiety for the fate of the child, which she sacrifices in the name of salvation. We see not just Madonna, but a woman, mother who suffers, like every man. In creating a generalized image perfect man - the main sense of creativity Rafael.
