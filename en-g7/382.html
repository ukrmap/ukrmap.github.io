Climatic zones and areas of the world (compendium)

Climatic zones - a latitudinal bands, each with relatively homogeneous climate, as determined by the amount of solar radiation and prevailing air masses. There main and transitional climate zones.

main climatic zones ( equatorial, tropical, moderate, arctic and Antarctic ) characteristic of domination over the year one of the zonal types of air masses. Transition zones (or subpoyasy) characterized seasonal dominance of different air masses coming from the neighboring main zones.

Depending on the set of indicators of climate (average temperature, annual amplitude of temperatures, the amount and mode of precipitation, prevailing winds) distinguish types climate. In addition, due to significant differences in climate, marine and continental areas within the same climatic zone variations distinguish the main types of climate - climatic region ( desert and wet - a tropical climate, Continental, Mediterranean, monsoonal, with a uniform damp - sub-tropical climate, continental, temperate continental, maritime, monsoonal - temperate climate).

The map presented climatic zones and areas with the characteristics of their basic conditions: solar radiation, prevailing air masses, seasonal temperature fluctuations.
