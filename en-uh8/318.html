battle of Poltava in 1709

  • spring of 1709 Charles XII of resumed hostilities. He decided to launch attack on Moscow. The way the Swedes had prolyahty through Kharkov and Kursk.
  • To proceed according to plan operations, the Swedish army had to defuse a well-fortified Poltava.

  • Meanwhile, the city came to Peter with the main forces.

Poltava battle changed the political map of Europe. The victory of Peter I of Russia has put forward one of the leading contemporary European politics. Power of Sweden and Poland actually been undermined forever.

For Ukraine, Poltava battle effects were extremely harsh: the Russian government, which won, was on the road even more limited autonomy Hetmanate.
