Most global problems have been exacerbated and especially in the middle of the twentieth century. due to the rapid increase in world population and rapid industrial development.

Global problems are divided into political, economic, demographic, social and environmental. Is most important is the political problems of humanity: an arms race, religious and resolution of regional military and political conflicts in different parts of the world and more. Recently acquired acute problem of combating terrorism.

global problem of war and peace came with the advent of weapons of mass destruction: chemical, biological and nuclear. Officially, there are five countries that have nuclear weapons: U.S., Russia, China, France, Great Britain. These countries are the guarantors of peace and world security. However, there are states (India, Pakistan, Israel, North Korea) that contrary to international norms already have nuclear weapons, or closely approached its possession. Taking care of the interests of humanity in 1994 from nuclear weapons first refused to Ukraine, which at that time owned the third largest nuclear potential in the world. Followed by Ukraine of nuclear weapons abandoned Kazakhstan and Belarus.
