agroclimatic resources - the ratio of heat, moisture, light needed for growing crops. They are determined by geographical location within the territory of climatic zones and natural areas. Characterize the agro-climatic resources of three parameters.

sum of active air temperatures (total average daily temperatures higher than 10 ° C) in which plants are developing fastest.

active period with temperatures (vegetation period) during which temperatures are favorable for plant growth. Distinguish short and long periods serednotryvalyy vegetation;

Provision plants damp (determined by the ratio of moisture).

moisturizing factor - determined by the ratio of heat and moisture in a particular area and is calculated as the ratio of rainfall to vyparovuvanosti year. The higher the temperature, the greater vyparovuvanist and therefore less moisture ratio. The smaller the coefficient of moisture, the drier climate.
