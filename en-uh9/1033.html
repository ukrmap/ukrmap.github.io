1. The emergence of organizations in the Decembrist Ukraine

Opposition sentiment that distributed to the Russian imperial elite, found expression in the emergence of secret organizations among army officers. Members of these organizations called the "Decembrists" by the Russian name of the month, which was their attempted coup of December 14, 1825 in St. Petersburg. On distribution of these attitudes influenced acquaintance with inhabitants of European ideas and social and political life of Europe during overseas trip 1813-1814 biennium Autocratic, feudal empire in order contrasted with what they saw and encouraged the nobility, who, after victory in the war felt own importance, decisive action.

Among the officers located at Right Bank after the withdrawal from Europe, 2 nd Army in Kamenetz-Podolsk there was a secret organization "Iron Rings" (1815). Company, a leader which was W. Rajewski, elimination of feudal united supporters procedures and the introduction of the republican form of government. After a year under threat detection company samorozpustylosya. Secret of Officers occurred while in other cities of the empire: in St. Petersburg - the Union Rescue "(1816), in Moscow -" Union welfare "(1818). Sharp disagreements that erupted between supporters of the republican form government and constitutional monarchy led to the collapse of these organizations.

In 1821 Right Bank, in Tulchin created a new secret organization of "Southern Company". Among its members were many members of Masonic lodges. The leader began a Colonel Pavel Pestel. A year later, in St. Petersburg, was formed "North Company, headed by Nikita Muravyov. In the ranks of both companies for 1821-1825 biennium there were over 200 members. Managers of both companies annually during the Contract Fair in Kiev, have held meetings at which discussed programming questions. Along with "Southern Society" in Volhynia acted "Society of United Slavs", formed in 1823 in Novograd-Volyn officers brothers Andrew and Peter Borisov.

2. Ukraine in the policy documents Decembrist

The program of future reforms "Southern Company" - "Ruska Pravda" produced Pavel Pestel. He project of empire had become a unitary republic with a unicameral parliament. However, for an unlimited period to implement these changes in the country military dictatorship introduced the "Provisional government" headed by Pestel. The people guaranteed basic civil liberties, abolished serfdom.

At the same time refused Pestel recognize the people to live in the empire, the right to separate existence as states. All nations were to merge with the Russian people, that is losing its national features. Future Power, in his opinion, should be centralized country because the federal system Pestel considered harmful. For Ukraine, which for it was only "Little Russia", no public rights is not recognized. Through its weakness - he said - "Little" can never become a separate state, and It must give up those dreams. The right to own statehood recognized Pestel only in Poland, and then only on condition that its close alliance with Russia.

Another vision for the future leader of the empire "Northern Society" M. Vorobyev. In its "Constitution" as he considered Russia federal constitutional monarchy with a march on the U.S. model. Serfdom abolished, carried out democratic reforms. Country divided into 14 federal states, two of which - the Black Sea with the capital Kiev and Ukrainian with its capital in Kharkiv created the Left and Slobozhanschine. All issues of domestic life Ukrainian states have the right to decide independently. In the center kept the right of a common foreign policy issue of money and creation of unified armed forces. Project M. Muravyova gave more rights to people, captive empire, however, the present state independence none of them received. States that they planned to create in Ukrainian lands were artificial in nature, since not all covered territories inhabited by Ukrainian, and tore it into several pieces of land.

In policy documents "Society of United Slavs" ("Rules" and "sacramental promise") proclaimed the abolition of serfdom and the democratic republican system. Unlike other Decembrist organizations Company put forward the idea of liberation of the Slavic peoples of the monarch autocracy and combining them into a federation of Slavic republics.

December 14, 1825, the day new oath of Nicholas I, who ascended the throne after the death Alexander I, the Decembrists revolt organized in St. Petersburg. But conspired to refrain from action and would force the Government to accept their requirements only for the most of his act of civil disobedience. They brought on the Senate an area of 3 thousand soldiers with weapons and waited to meet their requirements. Evening of new Emperor gathered faithful troops executed rebels with guns.

Two weeks after these events raised a rebellion Chernigov Infantry Regiment, located in Kiev. Government Retaliation against the rebels cast parts, January 3, 1826 defeated rebels. Prydushyvshy performances, the government ruthlessly Decembrists.

Decembrist movement was primarily pro-nature and limited Army. He revealed that social movement to Ukrainian lands can not win unless supported Ukrainian. Heated discussions that occurred in them, finding ways to release from imperial oppression, steel furnace, which shaped the views of representatives of the new Ukrainian intellectual elite - the intelligentsia.

In early 1826 secret circle appeared at Kharkiv University. Headed by his young students - Vladimir Rozalion nobleman and son-Soshalskyy Kiev merchant Peter Balabuha. 5. The Polish uprising of 1830-1831 biennium and Ukraine

In 1830 Ukrainian lands found themselves in a whirlwind of events connected with Polish nationalist uprising against Russian domination, which began in the Polish Kingdom.

Established in Warsaw as a result The national government uprising victory put the program revival Speeches Commonwealth's borders in 1772 In the Polish proposal to start negotiations Emperor Nicholas replied that the rebels may consider only two of his Summary: surrender or destruction. Then polish the rebels have put forward the famous slogan: "For Your Freedom and Ours!" They were counting on the union to fight against imperial oppression of all opposition forces from public Russian, Ukrainian, Belarusians, Lithuanians. On the Right Bank, they called on landowners at least promise to give land to peasants to engage them on their side, but most Polish nobility rejected this proposal.

In support of the insurgents were groups of Polish nobility of the Right Bank. Ukrainian peasantry believed their actions purely "business lords, and to persuade him to join Polish nobles, slogans alone was not enough.

The Russian government at first appeared in deep water, but very quickly created a way out. Chief of Russian forces, Field Marshal F.Osten-Saken addressed Ukrainian peasants Right Bank. "Do not believe them (ie Polish) - he called - and those who would persuade and compel an uproar, and try to grab dopravyty authorities. You never will belong to those landowners who stand up against legitimate authority. " Contents of this appeal, which was read in all churches Right Bank, farmers realized an opportunity to break free from the landlord's yoke. They gladly took the rebellious nobles, and handed them the Russian authorities. At the same time the imperial government decided, as in all previous battles, use military talents descendants of Ukrainian Cossacks. Appeared imperial order to establish the Little Russian Cossacks regiments in Chernihiv and Poltava provinces. Those wishing to join them turned out a lot, because it was stated that they will remain at the Cossack service. For six weeks Little Russian governor-general Nikolai Repnin (1778-1845) formed the eight regiments of 1 thousand persons in each. However, in suppressing insurrection Cossack regiments to take part did not have time.

Meanwhile, Russian troops in June 1831 suppressed the rebellious Polish nobility speeches on the Right Bank, and early September storm captured Warsaw.
